Matt Wujek

People that I work with know me as the approachable designer who advocates for users and applies business needs to create human-centered experiences.

They might also tell you that I care deeply about mission driven products that solve real problems like climate change, conservation, transportation, etc.

My design friends will complain that I can’t shut up about Figma, Lottie, and Mapbox.

Scoot

In the last three years, I’ve been helping Scoot shape sustainable, electric urban transportation. As the first designer at a rapidly growing startup, I had the priveledge of touching all areas of the experience from the consumer-facing app to marketing campaigns, to sophisicated asset tracking tools.

By fall of 2018, our team grew to just under ten designers. Since then, I’ve solely focused on the core rider experience. I have a variety of case studies that walk through my UX practice, design thinking, and craft.

Please send me a message if you’d like to see my work.

A little about me

I’m crazy about my tiny family (my fiance & doggo), Honda motorcycles, camping, drawing, weird fonts, pinball, and surfing.

I prefer to work with diverse faces, philosophies, and genders. I’d rather not spend 40+ hrs/wk in a workplace that doesn’t value and celebrate human differences :-)

I don’t mind if you look at where my social side lives on the internet. Here’s my Instagram! I also have a dribbble page where I occasionally post things.

Thanks for stoping by!
